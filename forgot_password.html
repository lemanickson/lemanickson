Step 1: User Name Verification

Fill in your username, then click the "Proceed" button. If the username is valid, you will be redirected to Step 2.

Step 2: Password Recovery Options

You can receive a new password through your email address. If you don't have access to your email address, use option two to fill out the IT-helpdesk form.

Option 2: IT-Helpdesk Form

If you don't have access to your email address or encounter any issues with Option 1, please fill out the IT-Helpdesk form below. Our support team will review your request and assist you in recovering your password.
